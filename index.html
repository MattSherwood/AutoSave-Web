How it Works

AutoSave is a custom keyboard extension that saves a local copy of your text as you type. If an app crashes or you accidentally delete a paragraph, just open the AutoSave app to recover your content.

  1. Install the App & Keyboard.
  2. Enable "Allow Full Access" in Settings (required for local saving).
  3. Switch to AutoSave when writing important drafts.

Privacy Policy

Your data never leaves your device.

  • Local Storage: All keystrokes are stored in a local encrypted database.
  • No Cloud: We have no servers. We don't see what you type.
  • Full Access: Only used to move text from the keyboard to the main app.
