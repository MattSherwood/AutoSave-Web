Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

AutoSave is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here's how we protect your data:

Local Storage Only

All keystrokes are stored in a local encrypted database on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud. Nothing is transmitted over the internet.

No Servers

We have no servers. We don't see what you type. We don't collect analytics. We don't track usage. We don't store any data about you or your device.

Full Access Requirement

The "Allow Full Access" permission is required by iOS for keyboard extensions to save data locally. This permission is used solely to move text from the keyboard extension to the main app for local storage.

No data leaves your device. The permission is necessary for the app to function, but it does not grant us access to transmit your data anywhere.

Data Storage

Your text is stored locally on your device using Core Data with encryption. The data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Encrypted at rest by iOS
  • Never transmitted over the network
  • Accessible only to you through the AutoSave app
